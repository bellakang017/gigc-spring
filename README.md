## Workshop Flow (60 min)

1. **Welcome & Ground** (8 min) — Icebreaker, frame session
2. **Find Your Instinct** (12 min) — Animal quiz, red envelope STORY prompt
3. **Build Your HIVE** (20 min) — Framework teach, writing, 60-sec partner practice
4. **Sharpen Your Thinking** (12 min) — Ask → Structure → Refine exercises
5. **Close & Next Steps** (8 min) — Synthesis, BCG visit preview
