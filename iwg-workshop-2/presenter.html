Story Framework

THE HIVE METHOD

Your story prompt: HIVE

H
Habitat
Set the scene. Where, when, what was happening?
I
Initiative
What was your role? Why did you step up?
V
Venture
What did you do? Be specific about your choices.
E
Effect
What happened? Numbers help.

WORKSHOP Activity

Use the worksheet + your HIVE story prompt

What landed? What needs work? Write one action on your worksheet.
