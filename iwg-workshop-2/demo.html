Example: How Bella Used HIVE

Turning Your Prompt Into a Story

Here's how I (Bella, your facilitator) answered my own Social Bunny prompt using the HIVE method.

H
Habitat
Set the scene
Last semester at UT Austin's Office of Career & Life Design, international grad students were attending career workshops but not connecting with each other or with employers afterward.
I
Initiative
What was your role?
I proposed Coffee & Connections — a low-pressure, structured-but-warm format where students could practice networking in small groups before facing real employer events.
V
Venture
What did you actually do?
I designed conversation prompts based on belonging research, matched students cross-culturally, and personally followed up with each attendee to connect them with relevant contacts.
E
Effect
What changed? Numbers help.
Attendance grew 40% across sessions. Three students told me it was the first time they felt they belonged in a professional space in the U.S. Two got informational interviews from connections made at the event.
